About us

Plainfield Fishing Resort is a 122 acre family owned and operated public fishing lake with 2.5 miles of shoreline. We have well kept grounds, park benches, picnic tables, swings, grills, plenty of shade, and clean restrooms. We have a full tackle shop with a wide array of live baits and a full snack bar. We offer Jon boat rentals with motors and batteries.
